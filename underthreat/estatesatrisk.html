Estates under threat

Over 100 of London's council and housing association estates are under threat of demolition. Click on the map below or the links on the left to view the estates by borough.

  • The estates have been grouped into the following 3 categories to describe their current stage in the redevelopment process:

  • Proposal Stage

    The estate is included for "redevelopment" in a Local Plan. There is no planning application yet.

  • Preparation Stage

    The estate is going through the consultation stage. It may be exempt from a ballot, or a ballot is pending or a ballot has taken place with residents voting in favour of demolition.

  • Implementation Stage

    The "redevelopment" has planning permission. The estate may be still occupied, with demolition pending, or a phased demolition may be underway, with some parts of the estate still occupied.

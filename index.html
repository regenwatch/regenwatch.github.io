Regeneration has consequences.

Research has shown the impact of displacement on communities as a result of council estate regeneration, often as a result of initial promises to residents not being kept.

Sadiq Khan's new regeneration policy

In response to several high-profile and controversial estate regeneration schemes, in 2018 the Mayor introduced new policies requiring schemes to provide:

  • 1. At least 50% affordable housing with no net loss of social rent.
  • 2. A full ballot of residents before proceeding with demolition.
  • 3. A right to return to the estate for leaseholders and freeholders.

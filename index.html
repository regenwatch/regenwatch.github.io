High Path estate, Merton.

Approved by the Mayor in Nov 2018. Scheme will see demolition of 608 homes replaced by 1,570 of which 20% affordable - all at up to 80% market rent. Scheme funded by Mayor and exempted from ballot requirement.

Received Mayor's final approval in Dec 2018. The definition of 'London Affordable Rent' allows rents of up to 80% market rent - scheme funded by Mayor and exempted from ballot requirement.

Eastfields estate, Merton

In Nov 2018, the Mayor approved demolition of 466 homes replaced by 800 of which only 262 affordable (affordable rent > 80% market rent). Scheme funded by Mayor and exempted from ballot.

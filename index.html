Estates under threat

An increasing number of London's council estates are being earmarked for redevelopment. We're here to ensure schemes comply with the Mayor's new policy requirements:

  • At least 50% affordable housing with no net loss of social rent.
  • A fair deal and right to return for residents.
  • Demolition considered only as a last resort

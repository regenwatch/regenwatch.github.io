The Mayor and estate regeneration

Since Sadiq Khan was elected in May 2016 he has approved funding for the demolition of 82 estates and exempted 44 of them from his own requirement to ballot residents on the demolition of their homes. He is also turning a blind eye to an increasing number of developers and housing associations providing inadequate rehousing offers and who are making up their own definitons of the various affordable housing tenures in replacement 'social' housing.
